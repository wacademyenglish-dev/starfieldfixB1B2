PHASE V: FUTURE PROJECTIONS

Predictions & Plans

WILL

Use for sudden decisions, promises, or predictions based on opinions.

"I think we will find life out there."

"We are under attack! I will charge the lasers!"

GOING TO

Use for prior plans or predictions based on current physical evidence.

"We are going to explore Mars tomorrow." (Planned)

"Look at those asteroid impacts! The ship is going to crash!"

PHASE V: FUTURE PROJECTIONS

Future Continuous

Use it to describe an action that will be in progress at a specific time in the future.

"At 0800 hours tomorrow, we will be traveling through hyperspace."
WILL BE + VERB-ING
